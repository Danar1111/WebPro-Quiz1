Sate Emprit

Sate Emprit is a typical culinary dish originating from Kediri, Indonesia which originates from sparrows. It's like marinated and flavorful chicken or beef satay, traditionally cooked over charcoal. What sets Sate Emprit apart is its unique peanut sauce, which combines peanuts, coconut milk, palm sugar and a blend of aromatic spices to create a sweet, savory and slightly spicy flavor profile. Skewers are often served with lontong and cucumber-onion salad.

Pecel Tumpang

Pecel Tumpang is a culinary specialty of Kediri, Indonesia. This flavorful dish is made from a mixture of vegetables, such as kale, bean sprouts, and long beans, served with a thick and spicy peanut sauce. What differentiates Pecel Tumpang is the tumpang sauce on top. Often accompanied by rice and krupuk (crispy prawn crackers), Pecel Tumpang is a local favorite that combines a variety of textures and flavors to create a satisfying and authentic taste of Kediri.

Tahu Takwa

Yellow Tofu or known as Tahu Takwa is tofu that is yellow in color, this is because the water in which the tofu is soaked is given turmeric or a synthetic coloring. Yellow tofu is a typical food of Kediri City, because many production centers are found in Kediri. know piety. Yellow tofu has a rectangular box shape and is slightly flat. This tofu also has a better density than white tofu, so that when you cut it it doesn't fall apart easily and the taste is more delicious.
